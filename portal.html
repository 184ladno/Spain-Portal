Transport Information

  • Barcelona: private coach from BCN to Hotel Barcelona Center
  • Madrid: private coach from Hotel Madrid Central to MAD airport
  • Meeting point is outside arrivals gate with staff signage

Intercity Travel

  • Route: Barcelona Sants to Madrid Atocha (AVE high-speed train)
  • Departure: June 18 at 10:30
  • Arrival: June 18 at 13:30

City Movement

  • Primary mode: pre-booked group coach for major activities
  • Walking routes are used for city-center landmarks
  • Students must stay with assigned group and teacher at all times

Travel Rules

  • Carry passport copy and emergency contact card each day
