What to Pack for Spain?

As you prepare for Spain, plan your packing carefully so you stay comfortable, respectful of local culture, and ready for each activity throughout the trip.

The Importance of Appropriate Dress Code

Choose modest and practical clothing for school activities and cultural visits. Bring layers so you can adapt from cooler mornings to warmer afternoons.

Dress Codes for the Spain School Trip

Wear clean, comfortable outfits for daily tours. Avoid clothing with inappropriate graphics and keep one smart-casual option for evening group meals.

Essential Items

Pack passport copy, refillable water bottle, power adapter, sunscreen, and a small day bag for daily excursions.

Recommended Clothing

Bring breathable tops, comfortable walking shoes, light jacket, and weather-appropriate layers for city exploration.

Medication

Carry prescribed medication in original packaging and keep a copy of medical details and emergency contacts with you.

Top Tips

Pack light, label your belongings, keep valuables secure, and prepare one checklist before departure day.

Your Health & Wellbeing in Spain

We take a supportive approach to student wellbeing across the Spain trip, from daily routines to on-the-ground safety planning. Your group leaders and local partners are here to help you stay healthy, confident, and prepared throughout the journey.

Keeping Healthy in Spain

Stay hydrated, use sun protection, and keep a steady sleep routine so you can enjoy each activity with energy and focus.

Mental Wellbeing on Trip

Travel can feel intense at times. Speak with staff early if you feel overwhelmed so we can provide support and practical guidance.

Safety & Security

Keep valuables secure, stay with your group, and follow meeting points and check-in times shared by your trip leaders.

Water Safety Policy

Only join approved water-based activities with supervision, and always follow local guidance and instructor safety rules.

FAQ

Questions about medication, wellbeing support, or daily procedures can be raised anytime with staff before and during the trip.
